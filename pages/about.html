A small body of determined spirits fired by an unquenchable faith in their mission can alter the course of history Mahatma Gandhi

These words are as relevant in today’s world as they were during India’s struggle for Independence with the view of achieving an inclusive development. Many sections of the society were looped out as India moved to new stages of development. To create a sustainable society, we must ensure the progress of these integral elements.

We aim at reaching atleast 1 Million Auto-Rickshaw drivers by the year 2020. We have no speck of doubt that we will be able to reach and surpass our milestones with the relentless efforts, faith and sense of purpose that we have demonstrated so far and pledge to continue it even in the future. InMotion’s approach in achieving the progress of the Auto-Rickshaw industry is multi-faceted, the prime being the Bachat - Swasthaya - Gyan (Meaning Savings - Healthcare - Education) Initiative.




Vision
Empower the auto-rickshaw drivers and bring dignity back in their lives.
Mission
To connect with over a million drivers by 2020.
