Inmotion Foundation

India’s indigenous three-wheeler taxi has served the average citizen’s chariot for over half a century. But these harbingers of punctuality and service do not have much to speak of themselves. In today’s world of uncertainty, savings have become all the more inevitable however after paying for rent of the rickshaw whatever little is left for them is hardly enough to make both ends meet. Lack of savings put their and their families’ future in jeopardy. Inmotion foundation aims at securing the life of India’s travel frontrunner by giving them financial security, access to affordable healthcare and education facilities.
